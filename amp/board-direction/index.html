UMS3033/US001

Support services at IECB consist of staffs in administration and finance, infrastructure and maintenance, as well as 10 permanent technicians dedicated to IECB’s technology platform.
The support services unit UMS3033/US001 is jointly funded by the CNRS, the Inserm and the Université of Bordeaux, and receives financial support from the Nouvelle Aquitaine Regional Council. Research teams also contribute to  financing those general services.
