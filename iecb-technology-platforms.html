IECB Technology Platforms

IECB Publisher 31 juillet 2020 IECB

IECB technology platforms bring together the scientific instrumentation and expertise available at the institute.

Open to companies and acadamic labs, they provide services in Structural biology and Preparative and analytical techniques.

 

IECB technology platforms are part of IECB's support services unit (UMS3033/US001). IECB's support services, which also comprises mutualised services in administration, infrastructure and informatics, are aimed at facilitating the work of the IECB teams.
IECB's support services unit is jointly funded by the CNRS, the Inserm, the Université of Bordeaux , and receives financial support from the Aquitaine Regional Council. Research teams also contribute to financing those general services.
