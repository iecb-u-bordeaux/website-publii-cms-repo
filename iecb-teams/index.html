Education & Science Promotion

iGEM

Since 2012, D. Dupuy supervises Bordeaux iGEM team to encourage students to focus on Synthetic biology. Every year, 20-25 students from Universities and Bordeaux engineering school, form a team and develop a project all year long which is then brought to a successful conclusion within the framework of voluntary internships during summer. Work results are then present at the occasion of a big conference in Boston that met ~ 400 teams coming from all over the world. (www.igem.org)
