IECB Logo Evolution 2020

Logo comment

The square of the logo and the typography of the acronym are interwoven and merge into a square shape.

The initial colour strips are assiociated to create a notion of movement. The cutting of some strips evokes a DNA sequencing of the genome in relation to the world of science and the infinitely small.

The font typeface chosen is Space Mono Regular to evoke the world of high technology using binary digital hardware.

Please respect the Logo minimal space secure. Distance = 1xE between the anothers elements.
